Simple meditation practice

Follow these steps for simple seated meditation:

  1. Find a quiet and safe place
  2. Set your alarm for 5 to 10 minutes
  3. Take a seat and try to keep your posture
  4. Close your eyes, scan your body and notice how it feels
  5. Observe your breath: Notice how the air feels in your nostrals flowing in and out
  6. If your mind wanders, don't judge it or cling to it... just notice it and gently quide your attention back to your breath
  7. When the time is up, come back slowly
  8. Enjoy the feeling!
